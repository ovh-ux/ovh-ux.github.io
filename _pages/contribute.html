How to help ?

You can help us in many ways and we'll be happy to gather your advice and your help :

  • Feel free to discuss with us on our Gitter .
  • Share with us your improvement ideas, tell us about a bug you've found by creating an issue.
  • Contribute to our open source interfaces and components on Github .

Don't hesitate : we'll always do better together ;)
